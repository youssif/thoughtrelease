This is My Thought Release

Written by Youssif Abdulhamid on July 1, 2013.

11:13 PM, procrastinating a restful night’s sleep. My thoughts slip back four months. Four months ago, when the only thought I had about my future was whether or not I’d be promoted the coming summer.

Four months ago: my hair long, my face beardless. Frequent flyer miles high, my status at its ball-time peak. Weeks, days before I decided to scrap the script and rewrite it all.

My computer clock tells me it’s July now, and I find myself wishing I’d done a better job of following through on my promise to my friends, former co-workers, and family to keep them posted on where the wind would blow my life.

I also found that, while there is a lot of content floating the digital waves about starting a career right after graduating college, very little exists on what comes after "happily ever after".

So, I’m creating a retro-blog chronicling how I got from Point A to Point where-the-hell-am-I.
